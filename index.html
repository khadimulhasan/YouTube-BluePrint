Deconstructing the 2025 Algorithm

The algorithm's prime directive is maximizing viewer satisfaction to extend platform session time. Your goal is to align with this by creating content that deeply satisfies your target viewer, built on three core pillars.

Personalization

Builds a detailed viewer profile to serve hyper-relevant content based on individual user behavior.
